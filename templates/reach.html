Legislation overview
LEGISLATION NAME
REACH Regulation (EC) No 1907/2006
APPLICABLE TO
European Economic Area
Northern Ireland
PURPOSE

High protection level from chemical risks, enhance innovation, competitiveness.

SCOPE

Production, use of chemicals and their impacts on health, environment.

KEY REQUIREMENTS

Registration, evaluation, authorization, restriction of chemicals.

PENALTIES

Effective, proportionate, dissuasive penalties by Member States

EXEMPTIONS

Certain exemptions for types of substances

GUIDANCE

ECHA provides extensive guidance documents, tools, and databases to support compliance with REACH requirements.
